Dosa Recipe

Dosa a healthy protein rich breakfast.

Dosa is a popular south indian thin crepe made with fermented rice and lentil batter

Ingredients

  • urad dal
  • chana dal
  • methi seeds

Intsructions

  1. wash all the ingredients
  2. put all the ingredients in a mixie and grind to abtain a smooth batter
  3. pour the batter onto a hot iron cooking utensil
