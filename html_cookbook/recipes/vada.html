Vada Recipe

Vada are crispy,fluffy & delicious donut shaped lentil fritters from South India.

Till day,traditionally made vada are offered to Dieties during puja.

Ingredients

  • urad dal
  • chana dal
  • methi seeds

Intsructions

  1. wash all the ingredients
  2. make the batter
  3. make the vada
